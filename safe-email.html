Safe Email and Web Use

Recognize and avoid suspicious messages and websites. Verify sender addresses, don't click unknown links, and never share sensitive information over email. Use spam filters and updated antivirus.

Safe Email Practices

  • Verify the sender
  • Be cautious of urgent language
  • Don't click unknown links
  • Don't download unexpected attachments

Warning Signs

  • Unknown sender
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Spelling and grammar errors
  • Requests for personal information
