How to Protect Yourself Online

Protecting yourself online is essential in today’s digital world. Cyber threats are constantly evolving, but by following a few key practices, you can greatly reduce your risk and stay safe while browsing, shopping, or communicating online.

Create Strong Passwords

  • Use 12+ characters
  • Mix upper and lower case, numbers, symbols
  • Avoid common words
  • Use a password manager

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  • Log into account
  • Find security/2FA settings
  • Choose verification method and link device
  • Verify it works

Keep Software Updated

  • Enable automatic updates
  • Check regularly for updates
  • Update browsers, drivers, plugins

Use Antivirus & Firewall

Antivirus software helps detect and remove malicious programs, while firewalls block unauthorized access to your device or network.

Avoid Suspicious Links

Never click links from unknown emails, messages, or websites. Always verify the source before interacting.

Secure Your Wi-Fi

Use a strong password for your home network and avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking.

Real-World Tips

  • Log out of accounts on shared devices
  • Do not reuse passwords across sites
  • Check website URLs before entering information
  • Back up important files regularly

These simple habits can prevent most cyber attacks and help you stay in control of your personal data.

Quick Safety Checklist

  • ✔ Use strong, unique passwords
  • ✔ Turn on 2FA for all accounts
  • ✔ Keep software updated
  • ✔ Avoid unknown links and attachments
  • ✔ Use antivirus protection
