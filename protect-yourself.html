How to Protect Yourself Online

Protecting yourself online is essential. Use strong unique passwords, enable multi-factor authentication (2FA), keep software updated, avoid suspicious links, and use reliable antivirus and firewall protection.

Create Strong Passwords

  • Use 12+ characters
  • Mix upper and lower case, numbers, symbols
  • Avoid common words
  • Use a password manager

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  • Log into account
  • Find security/2FA settings
  • Choose verification method and link device
  • Verify it works

Keep Software Updated

  • Enable automatic updates
  • Check regularly for updates
  • Update browsers, drivers, plugins
