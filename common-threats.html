Common Online Threats

What Is a Cyber Threat?

Cyber threats are malicious activities that target computers, networks, and digital systems to steal data, disrupt operations, or cause damage. As individuals and organizations rely more on the internet, the risk of cyber attacks continues to grow.

Phishing

Phishing is when attackers impersonate trusted people or organizations to trick individuals into revealing sensitive info such as passwords, credit cards, or bank details.

Malware

Malware is malicious software designed to damage systems or steal information. Common types include viruses, worms, Trojans, ransomware, spyware, and adware. Prevention includes antivirus software, software updates, and safe browsing.

Identity Theft

Identity theft is when someone steals personal information to commit fraud—monitor financial statements, use strong passwords, and enable multi-factor authentication to protect yourself.
